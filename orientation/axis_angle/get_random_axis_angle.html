get_random_axis_angle¶

ivy_mech.get_random_axis_angle(batch_shape=None)[source]¶

Generate random axis unit vector \(\mathbf{e} = [e_x, e_y, e_z]\) and rotation angle \(θ\) :param batch_shape: Shape of batch. Shape of [1] is assumed if None. :type batch_shape: sequence of ints, optional :return: Random rotation axis unit vector and angle [batch_shape,4]

hamilton_product¶

ivy_mech.hamilton_product(quaternion1, quaternion2)[source]¶

Compute hamilton product \(\mathbf{h}_p = \mathbf{q}_1 × \mathbf{q}_2\) between \(\mathbf{q}_1 = [q_{1i}, q_{1j}, q_{1k}, q_{1r}]\) and \(\mathbf{q}_2 = [q_{2i}, q_{2j}, q_{2k}, q_{2r}]\).

[reference]

Parameters

  • quaternion1 (array) – Quaternion 1 [batch_shape,4]

  • quaternion2 (array) – Quaternion 2 [batch_shape,4]

Returns

New quaternion after product [batch_shape,4]

quaternion_pose_to_euler_pose¶

ivy_mech.quaternion_pose_to_euler_pose(quaternion_pose, convention='zyx')[source]¶

Convert quaternion pose \(\mathbf{p}_{q} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{q}] = [x, y, z, q_i, q_j, q_k, q_r]\) to \(abc\) Euler angle pose \(\mathbf{p}_{xyz} = [\mathbf{x}_c, \mathbf{θ}_{xyz}] = [x, y, z, ϕ_a, ϕ_b, ϕ_c]\).

Parameters

  • quaternion_pose (array) – Quaternion pose [batch_shape,7]

  • convention (str, optional) – The axes for euler rotation, in order of L.H.S. matrix multiplication.

Returns

Euler pose [batch_shape,6]
